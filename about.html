About Us!

From a small house and lots of passion we started out in 2015. But our vision never changed. We still are strong on our commitment to great quality, hygiene and taste. Today we have grown from that small house to two large outlets in Vijaynagar and Siddharta Layout, we have grown from 50 items on our menu to over 300 today.If we have to credit somebody for this growth then it is You. Yes you! Thank you for being a part of our journey.
Today Sutra offers over 120 options in beverages and offers Mexican,Italian,Indian and Chinese cuisines with a sutra twist. Sutra over these years has branched out into brands today such as Coffee & Cream and The Bowl Factory.
